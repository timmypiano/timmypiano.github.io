ABOUT ME

I am a pianist, producer, mix engineer, and sound designer.
I grew up in Paris, France where I went to the Paris Conservatory studying classical piano. After moving to Nashville when I was 14, I learned Ableton on youtube
and it became my new video game. I have been producing ever since, and mastered more skills in audio production along the way.

Here is my soundcloud , with some archived works, no real projects, the real ones are on the way.
I value community, love, and music for the way it helps us escape collectively.

I went to Berklee College of Music in the Electronic Porudction and Design department, and hopefully will graduate.

I am fluent in French and English.
